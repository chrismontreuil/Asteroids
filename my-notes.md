## Graphics

### Combine SVG with programmed lines and strokes. Lines and strokes for procedural and behaviorial graphics. SVG for art that I design myself.

Inkscape SVG → Phaser SVG

     - Draw in Illustrator or free Inkscape,
     artboard 80x100px
     - Export as SVG, drop in public/assets/
     - Load with scene.load.svg('ship',
     'assets/ship.svg') in preload
     - Phaser renders SVG as a texture automatically
